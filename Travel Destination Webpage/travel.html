Things to do in Palawan

1. El Nido: Island Hopping Adventure

Enjoy a fun day of swimming, sunbathing, and socialising Experience snorkeling in pristine clear waters and see the vibrant marine life Make friends with other solo travelers, backpackers, and groups of friends Get free-access to a rooftop pool bar after island-hopping

By Haqqi Life

2. Underground River Day Trip from Puerto Princesa City

Get to see the amazing Puerto Princesa Underground River and experience the beauty of nature in Palawan. This 8-hour guided trip will take you to the longest navigable underground river in the world. As the place can only accommodate a small number of visitors daily, early booking is highly encouraged. An option to add an exhilarating zip line ride over the sea is available. If you’re arriving in Puerto Princesa via cruise ship, please book the private tour option as your timings require special handling. As such, we are unable to join you on regular or shared trips.

By Corazon Travel and Tours

3. Coron: Barracuda Lake, Skeleton Wreck, Twin Lagoon Boat Tour

Swim with the thriving marine life living off Skeleton Wreck Take a dip into Barracuda Lake and Twin Lagoon and experience what a "thermocline" is Taste a sumptuous lunch, prepared by your boat crew at a nice relaxing beach Enjoy a hassle-free journey with hotel transfers and snorkeling mask already arranged for you

By Corazon Travel & Tours
