Welcome to Olive Garden!

Craving classic Italian comfort food without breaking the bank? Olive Garden Manila & Buc-ee’s Menus features a wide range of delicious dishes—from creamy pastas and crisp salads to warm soups and decadent desserts. Whether you’re a fan of timeless favorites or looking to try something new, there’s something for everyone at Olive Garden. Perfect for casual dining, family gatherings, or lunch with friends, Olive Garden offers generous portions, bold flavors, and great value. Signature items like Chicken Alfredo, Lasagna Classico, and the popular Never-Ending Pasta Bowl make every visit memorable. Explore the full menu and enjoy authentic Italian meals made to satisfy.
